I am a dual degree student of Ph.D. in Urban and Regional Planning and Master of Science in Information at University of Michigan, Taubman College. and UMSI. For my studies in UMSI, I am specialized in big data analytics and computational social science.

I always want to try something new and never hesitate to share my knowledge and skills. You can find out more about my research experience and publications, teaching experience, my design portfolios, and some project-based technology blogs at this site.
